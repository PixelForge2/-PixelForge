How long does a typical website project take?
Most standard website projects take 2–4 weeks from brief to launch. Complex builds with custom functionality or large e-commerce stores may take 6–10 weeks. We'll provide a clear timeline after our initial consultation.
What does the free SEO audit include?
Our free SEO audit covers technical health checks, on-page analysis, keyword gap opportunities, competitor benchmarking, and a clear action plan to improve your Google rankings — no strings attached.
Do you work with small businesses and startups?
Absolutely! We love working with startups and small businesses. We offer flexible packages tailored to your budget and growth stage. Whether you're just starting out or scaling up, PixelForge has a solution for you.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Every website we build is 100% responsive and optimised for mobile, tablet, and desktop. With over 60% of web traffic from mobile devices, this is non-negotiable for us.

Yes! We specialise in website redesigns. We'll audit your current site, identify what's working and what's not, and create a fresh modern design that retains your brand equity while dramatically improving performance.

We manage Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Threads, Pinterest, and YouTube. We'll recommend the right platforms for your business based on your audience and goals.
